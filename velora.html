How it works

One platform for drivers, businesses, and operations.

1. Discover services

Drivers find fuel stations, washes, and service centers through country filters, geolocation, distance, and OpenStreetMap.

2. Reserve vehicle care

Conflict-aware booking workflows handle schedules, availability, time zones, vehicles, favorites, reviews, and notifications.

3. Run a business

Business owners complete verified onboarding, manage branches and services, and operate reservations through a dedicated dashboard.

4. Administer the platform

Administrators approve businesses, manage catalog data and reservations, export CSV files, and inspect audit logs.

Stack: Next.js 14, React, TypeScript, MySQL/MariaDB, Prisma ORM, Tailwind CSS, JOSE JWT, bcrypt, and automated production checks.
