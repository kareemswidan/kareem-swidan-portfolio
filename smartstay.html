How it works

A complete marketplace, not a landing-page prototype.

1. Discover a stay

Guests explore Palestinian destinations, view property details, save stays, and use AI-assisted recommendations.

2. Book securely

Availability is checked through hourly inventory slots. A unique database constraint prevents simultaneous double booking.

3. Manage properties

Owners use dashboards, upload property galleries, manage availability, and move listings through moderation.

4. Operate the platform

Administrators review properties, manage users, and monitor marketplace activity through role-aware workflows.

Engineering: PBKDF2 password hashing, SHA-256 session-token hashes, HttpOnly cookies, Cloudflare D1 relational storage, R2 uploads, and a free local AI engine.
